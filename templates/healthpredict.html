HealthPredict

Browser-run ML: linear regression predicts readmission risk score, logistic regression classifies risk level, and a content-based cosine-similarity recommender suggests care pathways.

Patient Features

How it works

  • Linear Regression — combines 5 patient features with literature-based weights to output a continuous readmission risk score (0–100).
  • Logistic Regression — applies a sigmoid function to the score to produce a binary High/Low risk classification with probability.
  • Cosine Similarity Recommender — matches your diagnosis text against a catalogue of 10 care pathways using term-vector cosine similarity.
